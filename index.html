About Us

RANE 2023 is the flagship conference in the field of robotics and an international event for researchers, companies and end-users.

Robots are no longer restricted to industrial environments. Today, the applications of robots have extensively expanded outside the factory environment. Due to the technological revolutions that happened in the field of robotics and sustainable engineering, the theme of the RANE2023 focuses on “Robots and Sustainable Development”.

This year's theme is centers around the advanced technological innovations in robotics and its applications in various sectors such as service, assistance, healthcare, inspection etc. including meeting the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Organized by
Centre for Automation & Robotics
Hindustan Institute of Technology & Science
